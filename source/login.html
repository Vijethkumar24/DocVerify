Don't have a MetaMask Account?

How to Use:

  1. Download MetaMask.
  2. Add the MetaMask extension to your browser.
  3. Log in or create a new MetaMask wallet.
  4. Change the network to Sepolia:
    • Open MetaMask.
    • Click on the network dropdown.
    • Enable "Show test networks" in settings.
    • Select "Sepolia Test Network."
  5. Go to Sepolia Faucet.
  6. Select Ethereum Sepolia.
  7. Paste your wallet address to receive Sepolia test ETH.
  8. Use the test ETH to upload your document securely.
